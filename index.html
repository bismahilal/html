About Us

DIET is the most significant educational intervention in the country, while the NCERT came up in the 1960's and the state councils of education resreach and training came up in the 1970's, the need for a third tier of training and resource support structure, right at the district level was genuinely felt in order to improve the quality of basic education.As result DIET's were setup in almost all the districts of all the states of the country.

Our Objectives

"The Aim of Education is the Knowledge not of facts but of values"
In the heart of dedicated professionals is the belief that the primary goal of education is the students achievement.The objective of education is to turn the eyes which the soul already posseses,to light.The whole function of education is to not put knowledge in the soul but to bring out the best things that are latent in the soul.

Our Mission

The Mission of the DIET is to provide academic and resource support to the grass root level for the success of the various strategies and programming being undertaken in the area of elementary and adult education.
